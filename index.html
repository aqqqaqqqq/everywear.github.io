We present EveryWear, a lightweight and practical on-body human motion capture system that uses only everyday consumer wearables‚Äîa smartphone, smartwatch, earbuds, and smart glasses with built-in forward and downward cameras‚Äîwithout requiring any explicit calibration. To support real-world training and benchmarking, we introduce Ego-Elec, a 9-hour MoCap-annotated dataset spanning 56 daily activities across 17 indoor and outdoor environments. Our multimodal teacher-student framework fuses egocentric visual cues with inertial data and is trained entirely on real-world recordings, avoiding the sim-to-real gap of prior methods.
